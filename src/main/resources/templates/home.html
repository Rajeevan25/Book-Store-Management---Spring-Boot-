Welcome to LKR Books

At LKR Books, we believe that reading is more than just a hobby—it's a gateway to imagination, knowledge, and empowerment. Whether you're a student looking for textbooks, a professional in search of insightful non-fiction, or a passionate reader exploring the world of fiction, we offer a carefully curated selection that caters to all age groups and interests. Our mission is to make literature affordable and accessible to everyone by pricing all our titles in Sri Lankan Rupees (LKR), with regular discounts, seasonal offers, and island-wide delivery. From local authors to international bestsellers, from children’s storybooks to academic research materials, our growing collection is designed to support your reading journey every step of the way. Dive into the world of books—right here at LKR Books, where every page takes you closer to your next adventure.
